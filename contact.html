Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

We offer a comprehensive range of shipping solutions including express air freight, ocean freight (FCL and LCL), land transportation, rail freight, and multimodal solutions. Our logistics experts can help you choose the most efficient and cost-effective method for your specific needs.

You can track your shipment 24/7 through our online tracking system by entering your tracking number. We also provide email and SMS notifications at key milestones. For complex shipments, you can contact our customer service team who will provide personalized updates.

Yes, we offer complete customs brokerage services in all countries we operate in. Our team of licensed customs brokers will handle all documentation, duties, and taxes to ensure smooth clearance of your goods. We stay updated on all regulatory changes to prevent delays.

Packaging requirements vary by transport mode and commodity type. Generally, goods should be packed to withstand international transit conditions. We offer packaging consultation services and can arrange professional packing for fragile, high-value, or unusual items at our facilities.

You can request a quote through our online form, by email, or by calling our sales team. For accurate pricing, please provide details such as origin/destination, dimensions, weight, commodity type, and desired service level. We typically respond with competitive quotes within 2 business hours.
